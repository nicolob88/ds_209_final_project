Data

The data used for this project has been extracted from kaggle from the US Energy Generation 2001-2022 dataset. It contains the amount of energy generated (in Mega Watts) by state, month and source. There are several sources on the file which have been categorized as renewable or not based on the list below:

  • Hydroelectric: Renewable
  • Pumped storage: Renewable
  • Solar: Renewable
  • Biomass: Renewable
  • Wood: Renewable
  • Wind: Renewable
  • Geothermal: Renewable
  • Petroleum: Non-renewable
  • Natural gas: Non-renewable
  • Coal: Non-renewable
  • Other gasses: Non-renewable
  • Nuclear: Non-renewable
  • Other: Non-renewable

The dataset has over 400 thousand records, but some of them belong to 2022, which we won't be including in our analysis given that the data for that year is not complete.
