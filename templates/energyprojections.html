Energy Projections
Projections
These graphs present projections for renewable and nonrenewable energy sources spanning from 2023 to 2031. The data highlights a significant surge in both the generation and consumption of renewable energy, with expectations that both will approach nearly 600,000 MBtu by 2031.

In contrast, the forecasts indicate a decline in nonrenewable energy generation over the same period, although it is still expected to surpass renewable sources, reaching approximately 3 million MBtu in 2031. Notably, nonrenewable energy generation is projected to show continuous growth during this timeframe, anticipated to reach almost 3.5 million MBtu by 2031.
